
Guinevere is the First Princess of the Holy Britannian Empire. She is a wasteful spender, using money pointless monuments and buildings. Guinevere feels that the Chinese Federation should be punished for embarrassing her brother Odysseus. She and most of the royal family are shocked to see Lelouch alive and appointed as the new emperor. 
She orders the guards to seize Lelouch, but they are stopped by Suzaku. Lelouch then uses his geass to make everyone there do as he wishes. Guinevere becomes a maid along with most of the imperial line.
